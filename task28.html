how to cook dal kichidhi

welcome to cooking world

ingredients to cook

masoor dal,green chillies,salt,turmeric,tamarind,tomatos

procedure

step-1:soak the dal for 30 minutes in water
step-2:now heat the bowl add the soaked dal into the bowl.
step-3:now add pinch of salt, turmeric,tomatos,green chilliesand tamarind to it
step-4:now close the lid of the bowl for 30 minutes.
step-5:after cooking for 30 minutes you can open it and smash the dal into smooth paste.
step-6:now you can take another pan add some oil and heat it then add some pices to it.
step-7:now add that dal paste to it and mix it well for some time and levae itfor 5 minutes

conclusion

Serve it with rice enjoy the meal
